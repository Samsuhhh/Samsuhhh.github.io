About me and my (magical) skills.

After graduating a rigorous 24 week fullstack development program called App Academy, I am proud to say I have deepened my programming toolbox. I know I have a long way to go and by no means am I the best programmer out there, but I'm willing to learn something new everyday and devote myself to getting better at programming, day by day.

Below is my current Technology stack, but I am confident I can quickly pick up any new ones given some time and little guidance.

Javascript

The language for those that truly know what it means to "Love yourself", because if you didn't, it would be best to stay away from this language.

The programming language of the GOATs. Simple, quick, and (supposedly) easy. Highly debatable on the 'easy' part but we grabbed this from a blog about Python.
